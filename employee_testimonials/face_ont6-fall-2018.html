ONT6 Fall 2018

From: Mark Oglesby
Date: Tue, Oct 23, 2018

Members of the Media:

Hello, my name is Mark Oglesby and I work for Amazon.com in one of their warehouses which are called Fulfillment Centers. In particular, I work at the Fulfillment Center Ontario 6 located in Moreno Valley, ca. We workers inside the facility have had our very hearts torn out by management’s callous indifference to our holiday bonuses which have been cancelled without notice by Amazon Corporate! To the quick! We workers will be hurting financially as these bonuses are normally used for the holiday season! Our loved ones! Especially our children and grandchildren will be wounded by management’s action!

In which case! We have decided that enough is enough! And we the workers of Amazon.com Fulfillment Center Ontario 6 in Moreno Valley, ca have let management know in no uncertain terms that if our holiday bonuses are NOT reinstated we the workers will walk come Cyber Monday! Hell! We may even choose to walk out on Black Friday and not return until after Cyber Monday! Who knows what other days in this holiday season may become effected as steam builds up and Amazon workers around the USA, Amazon workers around the globe also decide that enough is enough!

This’ a call to action! We need the support of workers everywhere to stand up and shout: GIVE THEM BACK THEIR HOLIDAY BONUSES ! Now one might ask: Is this action perhaps a little extreme? And we surely would answer: We the workers of Amazon.com were given no notice whatsoever that this would happen! We have been blindsided by this company Amazon.com! Plans have been made and so many are counting on this extra income much of which would go directly to holiday cheer! Extreme? Hardly! Amazon has been the one to be extreme here, not the workforce! What kind of a monster, short of Ebenezer Scrooge, would do such a thing? Jeff Bezos! The man who stole Christmas!

We are calling on all people of good conscious, indeed, fair play; to call Amazon.com and say plainly yet forcefully: GIVE THEM BACK THEIR HOLIDAY BONUSES OR WE WILL NOT ORDER FROM YOUR ONLINE RETAIL BUSINESS THIS HOLIDAY SEASON! We are calling all UNION organizations and UNION members and supporters to just: SAY NO TO THEAMAZON !

Also, we are asking the media to support this effort by broadcasting this action in both print and multi-media venues such as progressive talk-radio, pod-cast, UTUBE, Facebook, or Twitter; any outlet or avenue which would publicize and announce: GIVE THEM BACK THEIR HOLIDAY BONUSES OR WE WILL NOT ORDER FROM YOUR ONLINE RETAIL BUSINESS THIS HOLIDAY SEASON!

So please! Support the workers of America; those who labor day in day out on the main-street of this great nation! We believe in fair play while Amazon.com does not! We were given no notice about this action of taking from us our holiday bonuses, thus the holiday season itself! And therefore, we the workers of Amazon.com have decided to give Amazon management due if not proper if not notice: GIVE US BACK OUR HOLIDAY BONUSES OR WE WALK!

Thank you sincerely,

Mark Oglesby (Amazon login: moglesby).

From: Mark Oglesby
Date: Mon, Oct 15, 2018

Open Letter to Amazon.com Management from a Tier I Fulfillment Center Associate!

This is an open letter from an Amazon.com Tier 1 Associate who works at the Ontario 6 Fulfillment Center in Moreno Valley, California. My name is Mark Oglesby (login: moglesby) and I and many of my co-workers are and have been for over two years engaged in a fight for worker’s rights within the Amazon.com employment milieu; an extremely difficult setting indeed! Our struggle continues to be for a better, safer working environment!

Seemingly out of nowhere, Amazon.com announced that its workforce would be receiving a pay increase of the $15 minimum! And with this, many voices throughout the nation cheered this pay increase as if it came from on-high! But was it? And this ‘but was it?’ is the focus of this Open Letter.
